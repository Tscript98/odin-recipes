Pizza

Let's turn up the heat with buffalo style chicken pizza!

This bold and flavorful buffalo chicken pizza is perfect for spice lovers and comfort food fans alike. It starts with a crispy 16-inch prepared crust layered with creamy blue cheese dressing, then topped with tender diced chicken tossed in hot sauce and melted butter. A generous sprinkle of shredded mozzarella finishes it off before baking to golden, bubbly perfection in just 10 minutes. With its zesty heat, creamy base, and cheesy finish, this quick and satisfying pizza is a crowd-pleasing twist on classic game-day flavors.

Ingrediants

  • 3 cooked chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 (2ounce) bottle of hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 (8ounce) bottle of blue cheese salad dressing
  • 1 (16 inch) prepared pizza crust
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  3. Mix chicken, hot sauce, and melted butter in a bowl until well combined.
  4. Place crust onto a rimmed baking sheet or pizza pan. Spread salad dressing over crust.
  5. Top with chicken mixture and sprinkle with mozzarella.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly, about 5 to 10 minutes. Let sit a few minutes before slicing.
